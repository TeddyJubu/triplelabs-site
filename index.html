The Reality

Most software is built for spreadsheets, not people.

Somewhere along the way, apps got complicated. Features you'll never use. Settings buried in menus. Subscriptions that lock your data behind walls. We think it should be different.

Simple by design

Every feature earns its place. If it doesn't make your life easier, it doesn't ship. We ruthlessly cut the noise.

Open at the core

Our code lives in the open. Fork it, audit it, break it, fix it. No secrets here. Data portability is a right, not a feature.

Modular by default

Turn features on or off. Plug into your stack. Build on top—or remix entirely.

Tools for the way you actually work.

We're making things we'd want to use ourselves. Keyboard-first, fast, and respectful of your attention.

We grew up on
open source.

Free code and coffee-shop Wi-Fi got us here. It only feels right to build in the open.

Open source means you own your tools—not the other way around. No lock-in. No "what happens if they shut down?" No surprises.

We're building for the long run, and we want you to be able to come along—or fork off and do your own thing. Both are cool.
